    Xbox Series X – The fastest, most powerful Xbox ever. Xbox Series X features 12 teraflops of processing power to immerse you in future adventures, current obsessions, and classic titles. Up to 120FPS deliver sensational worlds that demand a closer look while 3D Spatial Sound puts you in the center of your experience.*** Play thousands of titles from four generations of consoles—all games look and play best on Xbox Series X.

    Xbox Series S – The best value in gaming. Experience next-gen speed, 3D Spatial Sound, and High Dynamic Range with our smallest Xbox ever. Go all digital to store your games, saves and backups safe in the cloud. Xbox Smart Delivery ensures you play the best available version of your game no matter which console you're playing on.**** Games look and play better than ever on Xbox Series S.

    Xbox Game Pass Ultimate – Play with your friends and the greatest community of gamers on the most advanced online console multiplayer network. For one low monthly price, you get the benefits of Xbox Live Gold, EA Play membership, and release-day access to Xbox Game Studios and ID@Xbox titles. With over 100 high-quality games and fresh titles added all the time, you'll always have something new.

    Xbox Games – Shop for the latest Xbox game titles, top-rated favorites, plus great deals in the Microsoft Store online. New and previously released titles that feature the Optimized for Xbox Series X|S icon leverage the full power and technology of the new Xbox Series X and Xbox Series S consoles to deliver faster load-times, dynamic visuals, and steadier framerates.
