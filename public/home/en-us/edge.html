      GET STARTED

    Which version is best for your Mac?

    Microsoft Edge has been optimized for both Macs with Intel chips (most common) and Macs with Apple chips (some newer Mac models).

    Not sure?

    Find your Mac's chip type

    1. Click the Apple logo in the top left corner of your screen.
    2. Select About This Mac. A dialog should appear.
    3. Make sure the Overview tab is selected.
    4. Look for Processor or Chip and check whether it says “Intel” or “Apple”.
