    Meet Surface Go 3

    See the most portable Surface touchscreen 2-in-1 in action, and discover the perfect way to tackle everyday tasks, run your favorite apps, jot down ideas, and enjoy a little down time.

    Built for everyone, ready anywhere

    Browse, work, play, learn, and binge watch anywhere on a high-res 10.5” PixelSense™ touchscreen with adjustable Kickstand. Our most portable Surface PC is optimized for note-taking, sketching, and touch with Windows 11, and is an outstanding 2-in-1 value that can replace both your tablet and laptop. Available with optional LTE Advanced1 for hassle-free connectivity.
