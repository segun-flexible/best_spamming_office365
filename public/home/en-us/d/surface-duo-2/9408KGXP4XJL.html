    Overview Tech Specs

    More than a phone

    Do one better on two screens. Now with lightning-fast 5G1 and dynamic triple lens camera. New dual-screen experiences for Microsoft 365 await, plus Xbox gaming, streaming, and every Android™ app in the Google Play store.*

    Do more with two screens


    Discover next-level multitasking that never leaves your side — apps, websites, photos, and Microsoft 365* — in a more immersive way. Make the most of two high-resolution touchscreens and light up new reading, gaming, and entertainment experiences.

    Do it all fast with 5G


    Set yourself up for success with instant access to the best of Microsoft 365 and every Android™ app in the Google Play store*— using them with virtually no lag, quicker load times, and a better, more stable connection in 5G.1

    A camera built for your creativity


    With the new, dynamic triple lens camera always close at hand, instantly capture vibrant, high-quality photos and 4K video across two screens, while viewing, editing, and delivering them across your devices.

    Ultra-thin, flexible, and designed to inspire


    Incredibly thin, two new high-resolution 5.8” PixelSense™ Fusion Displays open to an expansive 8.3” with a Revolutionary Hinge that adapts to all the ways you get things done, wherever you are. 

    Your favorite apps, always by your side

    Position apps, websites, photos, Microsoft 365,* and all the Android apps* in the Google Play store the way you want – more immersively across two high-resolution touchscreens.

    • Apps work smarter on Surface Duo 2

      Grab that to-do list. Plan your shopping. Drag an appointment over to Outlook email.* Drag and drop to move images, text, files, and more between supported apps in a way that’s easy, smooth, and fast.

    • Discover apps built for two screens

      Light up new reading, gaming, music, and ways to connect with enhanced apps like Microsoft Teams that make the most of two screens.

    • A page-turning combo

      Bring the latest page turner to Surface Duo 2 with the Kindle app. Optimized for two screens, lose yourself in a great read, viewing two pages at once and flipping through.

    • Entertainment anywhere

      Set Surface Duo 2 in Tent mode and immerse yourself in entertainment wherever you choose. Find your escape in a favorite TV show or movie on Disney+, streamed in an instant.* The Mandalorian now streaming on Disney+.

    • Next-level note-taking and sketching

      Reply to an Outlook email with a quick sketch by pairing Surface Slim Pen 2^ with Surface Duo 2 for exceptional on-screen writing across Microsoft 365* and Android™ apps on two screens.

    • Never miss a trend

      Discover the videos you love on one screen, while exploring trends and the hottest hashtags on the other with TikTok*.

    • View two sites, side by side

      Open and view two websites on Microsoft Edge at the same time to compare content and get inspired.

    • Catch up, share, and listen

      Share what you’re up to and see what’s new on Instagram, while listening to your favorite Spotify playlists.*

    • Call, order, navigate, and pick up

      Get where you’re going with help from Waze,* while making hands-free calls. Call and order takeout, while finding the restaurant in Waze* to make sure you get there while it’s still hot.

    • Create your own combos with App Groups

      Do things your way by pairing and launching any two apps at the same time with one touch. Try Headspace with OneNote to combine meditation and journaling to capture your insights.*

    • Asphalt 9: Legends, reimagined

      For the first time, experience Asphalt 9* enhanced for two-screen play. Get in gear and take on the world’s best, most fearless street auto racer pros to become the next Asphalt Legend.

    5G speed connects you to cloud services, entertainment, gaming, and more – wherever you are, in an instant.1

    Instant productivity with the apps you need

    Connect, collaborate and respond in real time with Microsoft 365* productivity apps, together with intelligent cloud services powered by 5G1 across two screens.

    Harness the power of 5G gaming

    Immerse yourself in epic interactive stories and connect with friends anywhere, with Xbox Game Pass Ultimate* and an on-screen controller that’s always with you. 

    Stream on one or two screens

    No WiFi necessary. Find your escape in a favorite TV show, sports, or movie streamed in a blink thanks to ultra-fast 5G.1

    Stay in the know, even when it’s closed

    Get notified of incoming calls and messages, adjust the volume, and plug in to check battery life while charging. See it all on the spine as it lights up with color indicators, icons, and more.

    Transform everyday moments into extraordinary memories. Surface Duo 2 makes it easy. Zoom in from 0.5x to 10x using all three camera lenses, while optical image stabilization keeps everything in focus.

    Three ways to take amazing photos

    Wide angle, telephoto, or ultra-wide. With the new dynamic triple lens camera, featuring high resolution, exceptional low-light performance, and bigger field of depth, you choose how much area you want in your photos.

    Review in real time

    View on two screens with a completely redesigned photo experience powered and backed up by OneDrive,* and accessible across your devices. Plus, span your camera app across both screens to view photos and videos.

    Make it perfect

    Edit photos and videos on two screens with built-in utilities and adjustment features. Then share photos, videos, and albums with friends and family, or showcase your creation on social media.

    Brilliant video

    Built-in front-facing camera is optimized for great video calls and selfies. Plus, make a more personal connection with Eye Contact that helps adjust your gaze on video calls so you can appear to be looking directly in the camera.4
