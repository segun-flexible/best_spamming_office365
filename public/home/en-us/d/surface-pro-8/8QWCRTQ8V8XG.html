    Overview Tech specs

    Expect more from your computer

    Surface Pro 8 is designed to light up the best of Windows 11, enabling you to work, play, and create in ways most natural to you. Laptop, tablet, or portable digital canvas. You choose – it’s your computer.

    Pause

    Industry-leading Kickstand at any angle

    Mark up, edit, or watch a video at whatever angle you need. The Kickstand adjusts nearly 180 degrees effortlessly – it’s like magic.

    Our most advanced display in a Pro

    Immerse yourself in the high-resolution 13” PixelSense™ Flow touch display. Now with up to 120Hz refresh rate, plus Adaptive Color. And, open your eyes to remarkable picture quality with Dolby Vision®.8

    Experience new Windows 11

    Less noise, clutter, and chaos. More creativity and connection. We reimagined the way a PC should work for you and with you. With Windows 11, there’s no limit to what you can do.
    Previous Next

    Our most powerful Pro

    Designed to deliver unprecedented levels of performance in an ultra-versatile package, Surface Pro 8 pushes the boundaries of what you expect from your computer.

    Intel® Evo™ platform

    Do it all with the first Surface Pro built on the Intel® Evo™ platform on select configurations,†† engineered for mobile performance with 11th Gen Intel® Core™ processors, amazing graphics, and all-day battery life¹ in a thin and light PC.

    All-day battery

    Stay connected to what matters all day with up to 16 hours of battery life1 and Fast Charging to go from low battery to full faster.

    High-speed connections with two Thunderbolt™ 4 ports

    Create your ultimate productivity setup with multiple 4K monitors, always have large creative files on hand with an external hard drive, or create a dream gaming setup with an external GPU.*

    All in an ultra-portable design

    Starting at just 1.96 lb.2

    • Console caliber gaming

      Buy a new Surface Pro 8 and get one month of free Xbox Game Pass Ultimate to access over 100 high-quality games. Play across devices from the cloud and discover your next favorite game with friends. Plus, get all the benefits of Xbox Live Gold and EA Play.

    • Life is better together

      Microsoft Teams* is a great way to connect – and on Windows 11, it's even more fun. Moms, dads, grandparents, and your BFFs are only a few clicks or taps away.***

    • Expression made effortless

      The new, more intuitive Windows. Say it aloud and have it transcribed with Voice Typing. Sketch it down with a stroke of Surface Pen.* Windows 11 has more ways to adapt to how you think.

    • The ultimate desktop experience

      Nothing’s holding you back with two USB-C® and Thunderbolt™ 4 ports. Go from productive tablet to Adobe* editing studio to ultimate PC gaming setup.

    • Award-winning shows and amazing apps

      The apps you need to work. The shows you love to watch. Find them fast with the redesigned Microsoft Store. Search for the show, game, movie —whatever you’re looking for can be found in one simple search bar. Disney+ coming soon to the Microsoft Store on Windows.

    • Access what you care about faster

      Windows 11 comes with Widgets and enhanced browser performance from Microsoft Edge—a perfect combo for performance and personalization.

    • Positively productive

      Quickly create and share content that stands out, manage your schedule with ease, effortlessly connect with others and enjoy peace of mind with Microsoft 365.3

    • The perfect way to view everything

      Surface signature 3:2 aspect ratio means you’re viewing everything perfectly scaled, with 18% more vertical screen real estate than a typical laptop.4 Plus, with Windows 11, arrange your apps into groups and optimize screen space with tools like Snap Assist.
